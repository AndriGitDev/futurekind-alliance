# Futurekind Alliance

A fictional/speculative **2040 international political alliance** website for **Futurekind Alliance**, based on **Freedom Commons Federalism**.

> Maximum freedom. Shared abundance. No domination.

The concept is an alliance of federated communities: free municipalities, cooperatives, climate-adaptation settlements, civic colonies, orbital/remote chapters, and rights federations. It is **not** a registered political organisation, active electoral campaign, donation vehicle, or voter-targeting operation. It deliberately contains no backend, auth, database, analytics, tracking, donation forms, or mailing-list capture.

## Stack

- Astro
- TypeScript
- Tailwind CSS 3 via PostCSS
- Static output

## Pages

- `/` — home and quick explanation
- `/manifesto/` — public-facing 2040 manifesto narrative
- `/principles/` — scannable principles
- `/policies/` — ten policy pillars
- `/federation/` — alliance/federation model and member community types
- `/about/` — fictional-project disclaimer and design constraints

## Content direction

The visual direction is solarpunk optimism, civic seriousness, clean European public-service design, and science-fiction dignity. The word “colony” is used in the fictional 2040 sense of consensual self-governing settlements and habitats — not domination, extraction, or imperial rule. Avoid real political party marks, copyrighted science-fiction branding, manipulative campaign dark patterns, and authoritarian aesthetics.
